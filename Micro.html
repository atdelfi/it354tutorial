Microservice vs Monolith

Monolith is what everyone back in the day was using as the standards. To have everything combined as one solid program. You would not be able to use any individual piece of that code without going in and copy pasting. Some strenghts for it are less cross-cutting concerns, easier to debug, simple to deploy, and simple to develop. Some disadvantages are, when it gets to a massive scale it is hard to understand, making changes becomes difficult, scalability as you can't scale individual pieces only the whole application, and new technology becomes harder to implement in the app.


Microservice is breaking an application down into many pieces to have multiple teams work on at the same time allowing for a faster end product. It also allows each section to have its own application and logic. Pros: Independent components, eaiser to understand, better scalability, flexibility in choosing tech, and the higher level of agility. Cons: Extra complexity, system distribution, cross-cutting concerns, and testing.


I personally think that Microservice is what the future is leading to as you can use other pieces of other applications in other projects, instead of having to completely start a new for new projects with Monolith.
