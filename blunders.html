Common Blunders

Small Clickable Areas

This makes the user have to be very presise to click a link on the page and having it large is more clear that it is clickable and easier to click. An easy solution is by adding padding to the clickable links as it makes the user more confortable to click on it.

Duplicate Page Titles

This is important to not do as it robs the pages from giving users information on if they are on the right page or not. Making the titles unique is important for search engines to see if the page is relevant to the users search. A good title would be along the way of the title of page and the site your on and possible a categorie for the page.

No way to get in Touch

User engagement is important to any website and not having a way to be contacted by users could turn off users and have them go to other websites. Having a contact page shows that you care about the users and be able to fix their issue will have a greater impact on them continuing to use your page.
