Font Issue

The issue was that Mitt Rommney used 2 fonts in his header so it started to cause a race to see who loads first causing invisible text for a bit.


This happened because each font has its own FOIT(Flash of Invisible Text) which caused Not to not show up in the offical Mitt Rommeny is not running for president.


It can effect everyones day to day life as you can go to look up information and see the title and base your knowledge off the title, like multiple people did off of the Mitt Rommney page.


Easy way to stop this is to have a default text be there until all of the fonts are loaded.
